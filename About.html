Our Story

From a Small Garage to a Global Platform

ShopEase began in 2018 in San Francisco with a simple idea: make online shopping better. What started as a small team of five passionate engineers working out of a garage has grown into a platform trusted by over 12 million customers across 24 countries.

We started by solving the problems we experienced ourselves — unreliable delivery, complicated returns, and poor customer support. Today, we partner with over 5,000 brands and sellers to offer more than 50,000 products across Electronics, Fashion, Home & Living, Sports, Books, and Beauty.

Our mission is simple: provide a secure, enjoyable, and trustworthy shopping experience. Every feature we build, every partnership we form, and every decision we make is guided by this commitment.
